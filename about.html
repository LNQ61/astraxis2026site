Company

Established in Singapore. Built on experience.

Astraxis International Pte Ltd was established in Singapore in 2025, built on a team with over 25 years of experience across Singapore healthcare, pharmaceutical manufacturing, and controlled-environment operations.

Why we exist

Bridging the gap between Singapore procurement and Asia suppliers.

Pharmaceutical and biotech procurement teams in Singapore face a fragmented supplier landscape across Asia. Astraxis exists to bridge that gap — providing direct access to screened suppliers, handling documentation follow-up, and acting as a single local contact point for time-sensitive requirements.

Our team

Our team brings direct experience from Singapore's Tuas manufacturing cluster and broader healthcare supply chain since 1997. We understand the documentation standards, lead times, and supplier expectations that matter in regulated procurement environments.
