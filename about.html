About

Not built from scratch. Built from 25 years inside Singapore biopharma.

Astraxis International was founded in 2025 — but the network, the relationships, and the operational experience behind it go back to 1997.

The foundation

Three family businesses. One supply chain capability.

Seng Leong Steel Pte Ltd

The origin of Astraxis's operational network. Seng Leong Steel has serviced Singapore's Tuas biopharma manufacturing cluster since 1997 — building direct working relationships with over 3,000 contacts across tier-1 facilities including Amgen, Abbott, GSK, Lonza, MSD, Novartis, Pfizer, and Roche. That network is the foundation Astraxis was built on.

U&P Oil

Shareholder and operational foundation of Astraxis International. U&P Oil provides funding and location support, enabling Astraxis to operate from Singapore with the stability and backing of an established family business.

Astraxis International Pte Ltd

Established in 2025. ISO 9001, ISO 14001, ISO 45001, and bizSAFE certified. The formal vehicle through which Seng Leong's operational experience and network has been structured into a qualified, documented, and scalable supply chain capability.
